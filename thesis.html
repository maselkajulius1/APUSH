Timeline Thesis

Thesis

Over the last 60 years distrust in the United states government has grown exponentially, with the development of a government that seemingly aims to control the world rather than support its people. Conspiracy and controversy increasing in density into the modern age, the perception of the american public of their government has continually become more skeptical and a more divisive subject. As time went on, presidents became more and more involved in scandal and conspiracy, with the stability and security of the executive branch wavering. While US presidents have always faced accusations of unprofessionalism, the constant publicized controversies of administration's following 1960 show a marked shift.
